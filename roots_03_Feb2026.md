# OLS team reading recommendation of the month, by Riva Quiroga
Planning your 2026 reading or viewing list? You might want to take a moment to watch Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie’s The Danger of a Single Story.

It’s a thoughtful and honest 18 minutes talk that gets you questioning the narratives we take for granted and encourages you to explore perspectives you might not usually seek out. It’s also a great reminder that the stories we embrace —and the ones we overlook— shape how we see the world. I hope it might inspire your 2026 list to go in some new or unexpected directions!

Check it out!
